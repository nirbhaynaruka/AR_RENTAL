Membership Criteria...
# A member of AR Rental Car refers to the person who has submitted his/her valid documents to AR rental Car. while renting the vehicle.
# The documents that will be held valid and accountable will be a valid Driving License and an Aadhar Card of the concerned person.
# In case of an international resident, a valid Passport, and an International Driving License (of his/her respective country) will be considered as valid documents.
# The concerned person will be named as a valid member only when he/she has agreed to all the Terms and Conditions and Policies of AR Rental Car.
NOTE: In any case, if any of the valid documents submitted to AR Rental Car are found to be fake at any point, AR Rental Car reserves full rights to take legal action against the member for fraud and forgery.

Eligibility Criteria...
# In order to rent a car, the person must be a valid member of AR Rental Car . (Read the Membership Criteria)
# The member must hold both a valid Driving License and an Aadhar Card, in case of Indian resident OR both a valid Passport and Driving License of his/her respective country, in case of a foreign resident.
# The member must be at least 18 years or above of age.
# Members must have had no alcohol or drug-related driving violations in the past seven years.
#There is no minimum amount of time that an individual must hold a license before becoming a member or reserving a AR Rental car vehicle.
NOTE: In any case, if any of the valid documents submitted to AR Rental Car are found to be fake at any point, AR Rental Car reserves full rights to take legal action against the member for fraud and forgery.

Vehicle Damage Policy...
1. In Case of Unintentional or Accidental Incident or Collision
# Unintentional or accidental damage refers to any kind of damage that has NOT been caused by the negligence of the member, to whom the vehicle was delivered by AR Rental Car.
# AR Rental Car provides unique liability insurance for all of its vehicles.
# In such a case, the member is responsible to bear the cost of damage up to Rs. 25000/-. Any damage above this amount will be covered by a liability insurance purchased for the vehicle.
# The damage cost will be deducted from the security deposit. If a security deposit doesn't cover the cost of damage, for any reason, the member will be obligated to pay the additional amount as an additional payment.
# The member, however, will be solely responsible to pay for any kind of damage done to an external property owned by any government or private authority, caused due to the incident or collision.
# The member will be solely responsible for any towing or impounding charges i.e. the member is owed to pay the charges for the towing van used to bring the damaged vehicle back to the pick-up point.
# If however, the member is found to be in violation of traffic rules or laws established in the constitution of India, OR found to be in violation of the terms and conditions of AR Rental Car he/she will be responsible for paying the complete cost of damage and will be treated under the rules of "Intentional incident/collision".
# The member is also liable to pay complete i.e. 100% cost of damage and service loss caused to AR Rental Car AR Rental Car due to damage if the insurance company deem the damage intentional or consequential (which will be stated in the accident report).
# AR Rental Car also reserves a right to take legal action against any member (apart from the fines imposed on the member) for the damage caused to AR Rental Car.
2. In case of Intentional Incident or Collision:
# Intentional incident or collision refers to the fact that the collision was caused due to the negligence of the member in complying with the traffic laws established in the constitution of India OR terms and conditions of AR Rental Car.
# In such case the member is solely responsible and liable to pay the complete i.e. 100% cost of damage and an amount of Rs. 25000 as a penalty for losses bear by AR Rental Car.
# In such a case, AR Rental Car reserves a right to refuse to apply for liability insurance claim to cover for vehicle damage and impose the complete cost of damage to the member.
# AR Rental Car also reserves a right to take legal action against any member (apart from the fines imposed on the member) for the damage caused to AR Rental Car.
